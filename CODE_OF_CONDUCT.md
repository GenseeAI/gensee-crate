# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Gensee Crate to be a respectful, practical, and security-minded
community. We pledge to make participation welcoming for people of all
backgrounds, identities, experience levels, and viewpoints.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risk.
- Respect differing threat models and deployment constraints.
- Give and receive technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Report security issues privately when public disclosure could put users at
  risk.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Publicly disclosing exploitable vulnerabilities before maintainers have had
  a reasonable opportunity to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit
participation from people who violate this code. Serious or repeated violations
may result in a ban from project spaces.

To report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers through
https://www.gensee.ai/contact.html. Security vulnerabilities should follow
`SECURITY.md` instead.
